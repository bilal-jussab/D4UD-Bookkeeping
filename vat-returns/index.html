VAT Returns

D4UD is here to assist you in fulfilling your VAT obligations. No matter if you're a newly established venture or have been operating for a while.

As a business owner, we understand that navigating the intricacies of VAT registration and compliance can be overwhelming. That's why at D4UD bookkeeping services, we provide a comprehensive range of VAT services to ensure that your business stays compliant with the latest regulations and tax laws.


Whether you're a new or existing business, we will analyse your situation and determine the VAT schemes that you're eligible for. We will explore the possibility of adopting one or a mixture of them, to help you achieve tax savings and reduce bookkeeping responsibilities.


We can prepare your VAT return, either by using your own bookkeeping records or by taking care of your record-keeping and VAT return. We aim to ensure that you claim as much VAT as possible, particularly on contentious items such as mixed-use expenditure and motor running costs.


As your Agent for VAT purposes, we can correspond with HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) on your behalf. We use advanced software that allows us to file your VAT returns with HMRC, saving you time and hassle.


Late VAT registration, errors, late VAT returns, and late VAT payments can lead to hefty penalties and interest charges. By entrusting us with your VAT affairs, you can enjoy peace of mind knowing that your business is compliant and avoid wasting your hard-earned cash on penalties and interest.


At D4UD bookkeeping services, we are committed to providing you with personalised VAT solutions that meet your unique needs. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you stay VAT compliant and achieve your financial goals.
